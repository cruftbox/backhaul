# Terms and Conditions — Backhaul

**Last updated: May 24, 2026**

Backhaul is a personal, self-hosted sports score notification application operated exclusively by its owner for personal use. It is not a public service and is not available to the general public.

## Use of SMS Messaging

Backhaul sends SMS notifications solely to the phone number configured by the application owner. Messages are triggered by sports score events (game start, score changes, and final results) as selected by the owner.

The owner has explicitly opted in to receive these notifications by configuring the application on their own private server.

## No Commercial Use

Backhaul is not used for marketing, promotional, or commercial messaging of any kind. All messages are purely informational sports score alerts delivered to the owner's own phone number.

## Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on the number of sports and teams configured and the frequency of scoring events. The owner may adjust or disable notifications at any time through the application settings.

## Opt-Out

The application owner may stop all notifications at any time by disabling Backhaul or removing their phone number from the application settings.
