# Privacy Policy — Backhaul

**Last updated: May 24, 2026**

Backhaul is a personal, self-hosted sports score notification application. It is not a public service and is not available to the general public.

## Data Collection

Backhaul does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data beyond what is necessary to deliver score notifications to the application owner.

The only data stored is:

- Sports team subscription preferences entered by the owner
- Twilio credentials entered by the owner, stored locally on the owner's private server
- A destination phone number entered by the owner
- A log of notifications sent

All data is stored in a SQLite database on the owner's private home server and is never transmitted to any third party.

## SMS Notifications

SMS notifications are sent exclusively to the single phone number configured by the application owner. Backhaul does not send messages to any other recipients and does not operate as a messaging platform.

## Third-Party Services

Backhaul uses the following third-party services:

- **Twilio** — to deliver SMS notifications to the owner's phone number
- **ESPN public API** — to retrieve sports scores (no personal data is sent to ESPN)

## Data Sharing

No personal data is shared with any third party. Twilio receives only the message content and destination phone number as required to deliver SMS messages.

## Contact

This application is operated for personal use only by its owner.
